Deforum Animator App Manual Summary:
[2A]
Deforum is an animator app that takes in commands for frames and natural language scene settings to create a video. The app offers various animation modes, motion parameters, coherence settings, 3D depth warping options, video input, interpolation, and the ability to resume animation. Here's a brief summary of the main features:
